China

Introduction to China

China(中国) is located in East Asia, and has the largest polulation among all the countries around the world, with around 1.428 billion in number in 2017. It also owns over 9,600,000 km2 land, ranking 3rd by area. The majority of people in China use chopsticks(筷子) to eat, speak Mandarin(普通话), and use Chinese Characters(汉字) as their written language.

History of China

China has a history of over 5000 years, and is considered as the cradle of civilization. Here is a list from Wikipedia:

Ancient China
· 夏 Xia dynasty (2070–1600 BC)
· 商 Shang dynasty (1600–1046 BC)
· 周 Zhou dynasty (1046–256 BC)
· 春秋 Spring and Autumn period (722–476 BC)
· 战国 Warring States period (476–221 BC)

Imperial China
· 秦 Qin dynasty (221–207 BC)
· 汉 Han dynasty (202 BC – AD 220)
· 三国 Three Kingdoms (AD 220–280)
· 晋 Jin dynasty (AD 266–420)
· 南北朝 Northern and Southern dynasties (AD 420–589)
· 隋 Sui dynasty (AD 581–618)
· 唐 Tang dynasty (AD 618–907)
· 五代十国 Five Dynasties and Ten Kingdoms (AD 907–960)
· 宋，辽，金，西夏 Song, Liao, Jin, and Western Xia dynasties (AD 960–1234)
· 元 Yuan dynasty (AD 1271–1368)
· 明 Ming dynasty (AD 1368–1644)
· 清 Qing dynasty (AD 1644–1911)

Modern China
· 中华民国 Republic of China (since 1912)
· 中华人民共和国 People's Republic of China (since 1949)
