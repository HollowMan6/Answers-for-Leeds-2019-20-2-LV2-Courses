Why Choosing University of Leeds

There existed a lot of alternatives for me to choose for my exchange destination, but finally I decided to go to University of Leeds. The reason why I select University of Leeds as my exchange destination is that I adore the international atmosphere here, I want to experience a completely different culture. Also, many of my seniors who have experience of studying here thought highly of University of Leeds.
